Mission

Today with open access to every person to the stock market we want to give regular people tools to help prepare people for the risks involved and help them quantify them. We have seen far too many examples of regular people losing their savings in the stock market

This tool is not a crystal ball. You will not lose what this tools returns, but it is a likely outcome that you should be prepared for

Methods

Historical Method

We take your stock and analyse the past 5 years of daily returns and look for the bottom 5% of returns
We then average those bottom 5% and look at what this gives us. We only want the worst case scenario
